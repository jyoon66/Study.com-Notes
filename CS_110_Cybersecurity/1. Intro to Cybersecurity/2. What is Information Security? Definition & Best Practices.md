### Lesson Summary
**Information security** is the process of making sure only those who are entitled to information can access it. **Confidentiality** means limiting information to authorized people. The principle of **integrity** means that information is accurate, and **availability** means that data is available to those who need it. The **principle of least privilege** means that people only have the access they need and no more. For example, a system administrator should have access to the operating system, but not the database management system. Security means more than just secure computer systems, but secure access overall, including physical access.
