### Lesson Summary
Information security can help you keep your information secure. There are four principles you need to remember. First, the CIA model. **Confidentiality** will help you remember to limit access to your information so that it's seen by only those you wish. The **integrity** principle will remind you to ensure that the information you're seeing is accurate and what you expect it to be. You'll want to ensure the **availability** of your information will be preserved. It's of no use to you if you can't get to it. Also, remember the last principle, **non-repudiation**, so that you'll know when your information is changed, how it changed, and who made those changes.


### Quiz

   * Integrity, Confidentiality, and Availability
2. Requiring you to sign a contract uses the principle of _____ to secure the contract.
   * non-repudiation
3. If your employer could view your medical records without your permission, what principle of information security would be violated?
   * Confidentiality
4. What are the four principles of information security?
   * Availability, Integrity, Confidentiality, Non-Repudiation
5. If a website requires you to log in with a password to change your address, this is an example of that website observing what principles of information security?
   * Availability, Integrity, and Confidentiality
