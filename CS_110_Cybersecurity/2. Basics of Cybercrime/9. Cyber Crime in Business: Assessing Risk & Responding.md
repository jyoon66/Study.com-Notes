### Lesson Summary
To recap, **cyber crime** is any offense where a computer is the target or instrument of the offense. This includes: **hacking, phishing, spamming, child pornography**, and **hate crimes**. For the hardware/software part of the system, ensure that you have **up-to-date versions**, a **secure configuration**, **strong passwords**, **additional secure software**, and that your **financial information is accurate. Prevention** is the best possible defense against cyber crime. Failing that, contact your local authorities.
