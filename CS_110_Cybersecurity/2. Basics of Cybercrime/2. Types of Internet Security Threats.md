### Lesson Summary
**Internet security** must be enforced to prevent criminal acts against our computers and networks. Cybercriminals invade our privacy by exploiting personal and professional communications. Often, protected copyrights and trademarked products are stolen to benefit others, while private and commercial servers are exploited for sensitive or proprietary information, often with the intent of destroying or modifying the contents. Additionally, the lack of internet security would enable **denial-of-service** attacks, impacting how and when we access computing resources. Identity information can be collected, and data integrity would be questioned.

### Quiz
1. In the world of internet security, the act of online monitoring for the purpose of intercepting and exploiting information is referred to as _____.
   * Wi-Fi eavesdropping
2. The internet security threat where zombie computers are used to saturate a server with unsolicited requests is referred to as _____.
   * Denial-of-service
3. In the digital world, precautionary steps taken to protect computing resources against cybercrime is called _____.
   * Security
4. The internet security threat referred to as 'pharming' can be described as _____.
   * Online fraud
5. 'Computer security' focuses on hardware and software, while 'Internet security' focuses on _____.
   * Browsers and networks
