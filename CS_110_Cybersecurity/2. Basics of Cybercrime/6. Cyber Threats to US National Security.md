### Lesson Summary
**Cyber threats** are considered to be by many experts the biggest threat to American national security. While the actors range from states like China, Russia, and North Korea to individuals who are simply anti-government, the targets are similar. Government information, the infrastructure and communications networks of the country, and America's economic livelihood are all at risk in this new cyber war.
