### Lesson Summary
Online security has been endangered since Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak marketed the Apple I personal computer. **Hackers** and their **cyber threats**, such as **trojans** (which look harmless, but aren't), **worms** (standalone programs with .exe file extensions), and **phishing** (unsolicited emails) threaten not just us as individuals, but whole companies by gathering personal information for exploitation. They can get in to our computers by pretending to be something harmless or by attacking computers with missing **patched software**, meaning new versions of software. They can cause big problems with companies by creating losses of millions of dollars.


### Quiz
1. What kind of cyber threat exists within its own executable?
   * Worms
2. What is the most common type of cyber threat?
   * Phishing
3. What cyber threat may occur when computer updates are not performed?
   * Attack on unpatched software
4. What are hackers?
   * People who gain unauthorized access to networks for profit or to commit malicious acts by acquiring confidential personal information
5. What is a cyber threat?
   * Any malicious act that attempts to gain access to a computer network without authorization
