### Lesson Summary
In 1994, **Vladimir Levin** gained world-wide attention after he hacked into a major United States bank and stole 10 million dollars using the internet. With the advances that have been made and continue to be made in technology, **technocrime**, a form of **white collar crime**, is on the rise.

Technocrime is also referred to as **cybercrime** and exists in many forms, including:

Computer crimes like **scams, social media intimidation, malware** attacks, **embezzlement**, and **identity theft**
ATM crimes like **trapping**, **skimming**, and **software attacks**
Cell phone crimes including **application misuse, Bluetooth attacks**, and **text message attacks**
Satellite dish crimes involving **piracy**

### Quiz
1. When someone inserts a device into an ATM machine to capture credit card information they are said to be using a _____
   * Skimmer
2. What is malware?
   * Malicious software that is designed to damage a device or steal data
3. Piracy committed by technocriminals using satellite dish equipment involves which of the following?
   * Illegally accessing radio or television signals
4. Which of the following is a common technocrime committed over cell phones?
   * Text message attacks
5. When technocriminals use computers to maliciously target people through social media, it can be referred to as _____
   * Social media intimidation
