### Lesson Summary
A few steps that people can use to protect themselves from cybercrimes are:

1. Making a secure password that cannot be easily guessed, or is not written down in an unsecured location.
2. Make sure your operating system and important software have the latest patches and updates.
3. Make sure you have Antivirus software installed and constantly updated.
4. Do your research on retail web sites. Make sure the site has encryption capabilities on financial transactions.
5. Review your financial transactions, privacy policies, and credit activity on a regular basis.
