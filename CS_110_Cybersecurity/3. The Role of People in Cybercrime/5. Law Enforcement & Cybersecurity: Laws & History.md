### Lesson Summary
In this lesson we defined **cybercrime** (criminal activity that involves a computer, network or the Internet). We also defined what a **cybercriminal** is (someone who commits cybercrime). While discussing the history of this phenomena, we learned about one of the earliest known persons to be labeled a cybercriminal. He was a man nicknamed Captain Crunch. We also talked about the concept of **cybersecurity**, which is a response to cybercrime in the form of laws enacted over the last few decades to help discourage and prosecute cybercriminals. Lastly, we discussed the unusual difficulties that law enforcement agencies face, when fighting this new type of crime. We covered some of the new departments and centers they have developed in order to fight this new threat called cybercrime.
