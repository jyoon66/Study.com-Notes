### Lesson Summary
Building a **cybersecurity** team is an important addition for all workplaces because a business of any size can be the target of a **cybercrime**. Members of the information security team may have varied roles in a smaller organization or more specific responsibilities in a larger one. Cybersecurity roles and responsibilities range from management positions such as a **chief security officer** or a **security manager** (who may be responsible for the **technology stack**) to **security software developers** or **cryptanalysts** who thinks like hackers as part of their job to thwart possible cybercrimes. The role of a **cryptologist**, for example, is to use encryption to develop software and tools to protect an organization's systems.
