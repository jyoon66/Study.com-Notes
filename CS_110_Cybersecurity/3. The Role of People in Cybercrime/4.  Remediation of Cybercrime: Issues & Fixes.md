### Lesson Summary
In this lesson, you have been educated on the real threat of **cybercrime** and the impact that it has on our lives. Five of the most common types of cybercrimes are:

1. Phishing
2. Identity theft
3. Hacking
4. Invasion of privacy
5. Cyberstalking

All these were explained in high level detail in an attempt to produce cybercrime tactic awareness. Finally, tips were provided to show you how to protect yourself against these common types of cybercrimes.
