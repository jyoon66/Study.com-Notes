### Lesson Summary
**Information security framework** helps to guide the implementation of policies, procedures and guides developed for the successful protection of information assets of an organization.

As a result of the successful implementation, customers are assured of their personal **data** and privacy security and consequently, improve their confidence in the services rendered them by the organization. It also helps in protecting the organization from network security **breach**.
