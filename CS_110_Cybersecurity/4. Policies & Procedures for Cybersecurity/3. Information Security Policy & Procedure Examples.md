### Lesson Summary
**Information security policy** is a set of framework policy documents created to guide the protection of a company's information and data assets from unauthorized access distribution and loss.

The **procedures** are the steps taken to implement the policies. In our example, Sum Payment Group Limited, enlisted the services of Big Up Security Audit to formulate its security policies and protect it from potential information leakage or loss.

Big Up Security Audit introduced several policies in this direction such as:
* Acceptable Encryption Policy
* Acceptable Email Use Policy
* Disaster Recovery Plan Policy
* Password Protection
* Remote Access
* Bring Your Own Device Policy
* Business Continuity Plan Policy
